About Tazerwa

TAZE is your smart online shopping and delivery platform designed to make life easier. From fresh vegetables, fruits, cooked food, and drinks to cosmetics, home essentials, and gifts — TAZE brings everything you need right to your door.

Our mission is simple: to save your time and deliver happiness, fast and fresh.

⚡ How TAZE Works

  1. Browse & Order:
    • Visit our website or WhatsApp catalog.
    • Choose from fresh food, drinks, home items, or gifts.
  2. Easy Payment:
    • Pay with MoMo or with Card
  3. Fast Delivery:
    • Our delivery team brings your order to your home, office, or school — quickly and safely.

    💚 Why Choose TAZE?

    • 🥬 Fresh & Quality Products – From trusted shops and farmers.
    • 🚴 Fast & Reliable Delivery – Save time and avoid stress.
    • 📱 Easy to Use – Order online or on WhatsApp in just a few clicks.
    • 💰 Affordable Prices – No extra costs, fair and clear pricing.
    • 🎁 Variety in One Place – Food, drinks, cosmetics, gifts, and more.

    🙌 Our Promise

    At TAZE, we believe shopping should be simple, affordable, and enjoyable. We work every day to connect you with the products you love — without wasting your time in traffic or long queues.

    Whether you’re shopping for your home, business, or hosting an event, we make it easier than ever to access top-quality products at competitive prices—without ever leaving your home. We’re passionate about helping Rwandans live healthier, hassle-free lives with dependable delivery and customized subscriptions for every need.

    Tazerwa isn’t just a store—it’s a movement to modernize access to quality groceries in Rwanda, support local producers, and empower healthier lifestyles. Join thousands of happy customers and experience the future of grocery shopping today.
